Collection Of Milk

Pure & Natural Milk, Delivered to Your Doorstep

Milk collection is the first and one of the most crucial steps in the dairy supply chain. It begins at the grassroots level, where fresh milk is collected from dairy farmers. The entire process is designed to ensure that the milk remains fresh, hygienic, and of the highest quality from the moment it is collected until it reaches the processing facilities.

Step 1: Milk Collection

Farmers collect fresh milk from dairy cows, ensuring it's kept in clean containers.

Step 2: Transport to Collection Center

Milk is transported to the collection center in refrigerated trucks to maintain freshness.

Step 3: Testing and Quality Check

At the collection center, the milk undergoes testing for quality and safety standards.

Step 4: Storage

Milk is stored in large, refrigerated tanks to keep it fresh until itâ€™s processed.

Step 5: Processing

The milk is then pasteurized, homogenized, and packaged for distribution.
